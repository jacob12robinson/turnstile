EARN YOUR SPOT IN THE ROOM

The FRI is a 0–100 score that reflects how reliable a fan you are. It's not about how fast you click — it's about showing up. Higher scores unlock access to higher-demand shows. The system is transparent, fair, and designed to reward real fans over time.

↑
Attend shows you claim
Every show attended improves your score. Consistent attendance over 6+ months builds a score floor that new users can't reach.
↑
Release tickets early
Can't make it? Release 72+ hours before the show and receive full score credit. Someone on the waitlist gets your ticket at face value.
↓
No-shows hurt your score
Missing a show without releasing your ticket is a score hit. Consecutive no-shows accelerate the penalty. Life happens — 2 emergency holds per year, score-neutral.
✕
No outside resale — ever
Listing a Turnstile ticket on StubHub or any third-party platform resets your FRI to zero. Tickets are non-transferable outside the platform.
